what is low level language ? and what are other levels and their respective meanings ?
---- ANS
*Low-level :::: languages (Assembly, Machine Code) are used for direct hardware manipulation.
*High-level :::: languages (Python, Java) are easier to use and more abstracted from the hardware.
*Intermediate languages (IL) :::: act as a bridge between high-level languages and machine code.
